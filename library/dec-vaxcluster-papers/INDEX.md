# DEC VAXcluster and Research Papers Collection

**Collection Date**: 2025-12-31
**Location**: ~/cyberspace/dec-vaxcluster-papers/

## Overview

This collection contains academic papers, technical journals, and documentation related to Digital Equipment Corporation's (DEC) VAXcluster systems and Systems Communication Services (SCS/SCA) architecture.

## Additional Research Directions

### DEC Research Labs
- DEC Systems Research Center (SRC) - Palo Alto, CA (1984-2002)
  - Founded by Robert Taylor from Xerox PARC
  - Acquired by Compaq (1998), then HP (2002)
  - Research reports available online

- DEC Western Research Laboratory (WRL) - Palo Alto, CA
  - Focused on microprocessor design, compilers, networking
  - Technical reports archived at HP Labs and bitsavers

### Related Technologies
- MSCP (Mass Storage Control Protocol)
- DSSI (Digital Storage Systems Interconnect)
- CI (Computer Interconnect)
- Ethernet clustering
- Distributed lock management algorithms
- Quorum-based consensus systems

## Notes

- The VAXcluster architecture represents pioneering work in distributed systems with strong consistency guarantees
- Systems Communication Architecture (SCA) provided the foundation for cluster-wide operations
- Relevant to modern distributed agent architectures with cryptographic security and eventual consistency
- SCS/SCA concepts influenced later clustering and distributed systems designs
